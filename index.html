        Mission          

Manhattan Downtown Bangladesh Community Alliance

Manhattan Downtown Bangladeshi Community Association, Inc. is a non-profit organization that started with a mission of uniting our community to help bring social changes to the world. We partner with various organizations locally for many different causes, focusing primarily on aid throughout the Downtown Manhattan area. On a community level, we provide a food and resources for families to bring hope to people in need and thus deliver social cohesion. On a global level, we hope to change our society positively and continue to take steps towards a better world.


Vision

Manhattan Downtown Bangladeshi Community Association, Inc. supports the betterment of our community and the world especially helping the underprivileged. The organization is not dedicated to any one cause but, is adaptable to all the needs that our community has. We will keep you posted as we continue to ​make our mission a reality.
