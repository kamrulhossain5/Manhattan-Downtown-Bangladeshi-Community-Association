PRIVACY POLICY

1) Contact Information and Preferences: We may request contact information such as your name, mailing address, telephone number, e-mail address, and your preferences for how you would like to be contacted by Manhattan Downtown Bangladeshi Community Association, Inc.. We may also use third-party services to supplement or to ensure accuracy of contact information.



2) Financial Information: If you make a donation to Manhattan Downtown Bangladeshi Community Association, Inc. or make a retail purchase on a Manhattan Downtown Bangladeshi Community Association, Inc. Site, we will collect payment information such as your credit card number to process your transaction. We may also collect the name of your employing company, if you would like for that company to make a matching donation. We may use a third-party payment service to process payments made through the Services. We will retain your credit card information only for as long as necessary to process the transaction.



3) Donations: If you make a donation to Manhattan Downtown Bangladeshi Community Association, Inc., we will collect Personal Information from you that may include: your name, address, telephone number, and/or e-mail address so that we can send you a receipt and thank you for your donation. We may also collect limited financial information to process your donations.

REFUND POLICY

1) Refund Policy: We are grateful for your donation and support of our non-profit organization. Donations are normally nonrefundable but if you feel that there is an error in making your donation, please contact us. If granted a refund, refunds are returned using the original method of payment. If you made your donation by credit card, your refund will be credited to that same credit card.

2) Automated Recurring Donation Cancellation: Ongoing support is important to enabling projects to continue their work, so we encourage donors to continue to contribute to projects over time. But if you must cancel your recurring donation, please notify us via email immediately.
